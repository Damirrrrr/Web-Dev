French Casserole

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 10
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken thighs 6 pieces
Unsalted butter 2 tablespoons
Cremini mushrooms 8 ounces
Garlic 3 cloves
Chicken stock 2 cups
Potatoes 1 pound

Step 1: Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.

Step 2: Season chicken thighs with 1 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper

Step 3: Melt butter in a Dutch oven over medium heat. Working in batches, add chicken, skin-side down, and sear both sides until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side; set aside.

Step 4: Add mushrooms, shallots and celery, and cook, stirring occasionally, until mushrooms are tender and browned, about 5-7 minutes; season with salt and pepper, to taste.

Step 5: Stir in garlic until fragrant, about 1 minute.

Step 6: Whisk in flour until lightly browned, about 1 minute.

Step 7: Stir in wine, scraping any browned bits from the bottom of the Dutch oven. Stir in chicken stock, potatoes, thyme, rosemary and bay leaf. Return chicken to the Dutch oven.

Step 8: Place into oven and bake until potatoes are tender and chicken has completely cooked through, reaching an internal temperature of 175 degrees F, about 40-45 minutes.

Step 9: Stir in heavy cream; season with salt and pepper, to taste.

Step 10: Serve immediately.

Shakshuka

  • Time: 5-7 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
Onion 1
Red bell pepper 1
Garlic 4 cloves
Paprika 2 teaspoons
Cumin 1 teaspoon
Chili powder 1/4 teaspoon
Whole peeled tomato 28 ounces
Egg 6-4

Step 1: Heat olive oil in a large saut√© pan on medium heat. Add the chopped bell pepper and onion and cook for 5 minutes or until the onion becomes translucent.

Step 2: Add garlic and spices and cook an additional minute.

Step 3: Pour the can of tomatoes and juice into the pan and break down the tomatoes using a large spoon. Season with salt and pepper and bring the sauce to a simmer.

Step 4: Use your large spoon to make small wells in the sauce and crack the eggs into each well. Cover the pan and cook for 5-8 minutes, or until the eggs are done to your liking
